We are Gwonzi, a group of nerds who love games

Commander

Our playgroup loves Magic, and most of all we love Commander! In our EDH series, we play in a season format where we earn points to compete over multiple games. Please enjoy our recorded top-down gameplay with post-match narration, and please leave us a comment every time we misplay. We consider ourselves to be competitive-casual: we try not to go too hard into cEDH, but we're also not piloting battlecruiser decks.

Tabletop Gaming

Board games are a wonderful social activity, and we have been branching out into the world of modern gaming. We haven't yet recorded any gameplay from our tabletop nights, so let us know if this is something that interests you. We've recently been enjoying Concordia, Lords of Waterdeep, and Castles of Burgundy. (Only Chris likes the Resistance and Food Chain Magnate, but he really likes them.)

Streaming

Completing the gaming trifecta, all of us are also big video game players. While none of us are terribly experienced in streaming, we're curious to see if viewers would be interested in seeing us on Twitch. If you are, please drop us a line by email or contact us through social media.
