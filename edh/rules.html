We play Commander in a season-style format where players battle over six games to see who comes out victorious. We adhere to the standard rules and banlist for Commander, and we currently do not have any playgroup-specific bans.

Rank Points

The primary method by which players can win a season is through Rank Points. Each player earns rank points at the end of a season game based on how they did:

  • 1st place — 4 Rank Points
  • 2nd place — 3 Rank Points
  • 3rd place — 2 Rank Points
  • 4th place — 1 Rank Points

The player with the most Rank Points at the end of a season is that season's winner. Because each ranking is important, one problem we wanted to avoid is "kingmaker" situations where a player gets to decide what position everyone else gets. For instance, if you have infinite mana and artifact tokens, Breya kills each opponent one at a time. In our playgroup, if a player demonstrates the ability to kill everyone without actually changing the board state for the other players (and no one has any responses), that player simply leaves the game and claims 1st place. The remaining players then continue the game as normal, competing for the remaining places.

Bonus Points

Rank Point ties are broken by Bonus Points. Bonus Points are additional achievements players can be rewarded for in-game, and come from being the first to do a certain type of action, accomplishing something rare or difficult, or continuing in the face of adversity.

We didn't want Bonus Points to completely warp our season format, which is why they are a separate point category. We love stats, so it's definitely fun to look at who won extra points and how, but we want these to function as tie breakers more than game breakers. Additionally, we are still nailing down exactly what we want our bonus points to be, so don't be too surprised if some of these change over time.

First Blood — First player to deal combat damage to an opponent.
First Counter — First player to counter a spell.
First Removal — First player to resolve a single-target removal spell or ability.
First Sweeper — First player to resolve a board wipe (for any kind of permanent).
Generalist — Cast your commander 3+ times from the command zone.
Flex — Deal 20+ combat damage with a creature in a single attack.
Generosity — Never attack the player with the lowest life total (except 1v1).
Commanded — Kill an opponent with commander damage.
Overrun — Have 10 unique creatures on the board at one time.
Wrath — Resolve 3 different board wipes (for any kind of permanent).
Greed — Draw 25+ cards (excluding your first card each turn).
Non-bo — Win 1st place without using a combo.
Hero — Prevent another player from being eliminated when you aren't at risk.
