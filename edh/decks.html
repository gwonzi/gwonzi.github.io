Commander Decklists

Take a peek at our 99

This page hosts links to all of our active decks that are ready for season play, which can always be found on MTGGoldfish. We usually have a couple of proxied decks on the back burner, ready to play casually or still in the testing phases, and those lists may or may not be shown here. Let us know by contacting us if you are interested in us making detailed write-ups of our decks!

Chris

Current version: 1.0

This Aminatou deck leverages her cheap cost and unique abilities to manipulate the top of your library and blink ETB value cards. Entreat either the Angels or the Dead for a huge board, or find a wonky infinite combo for the win.

Current version: 1.1
Past versions: 1.0

Chris's Breya deck plays a few infinite combos, and it uses heavy artifact synergy to gain value while assembling the pieces. This build meshes several playstyles together while providing many engines to get going.

Corey

Current version: 1.0

This Edgar deck gains an aggressive advantage from the eminence ability, and the added tokens fuel several synergies. If the vampires don't overrun the board, watch out for a few combos like Exquisite - Bond.

Current version: 1.0

Corey's Narset deck uses extra turns and additional combats to swing in with the hexproof commander again and again. This creatureless deck plays a heavy control strategy to survive until Narset can stick.

Eduardo

Current version: 1.0

Merfolk tribal meets Voltron in Eduardo's Kumena deck. This list has a big subtheme of tap-untap shenanigans, which means he can really get going when Paradox Engine hits the board.

Current version: 1.0

Big, big mana makes Omnath a big, big boy. This mono-green deck wants to generate enormous amounts of mana so Omnath can smack you in the face, then sink it into a value engine. With untappers, this deck can find infinite mana in several ways.

Ryan

Current version: 1.0

Ryan's Goblin deck is a mono-red Krenko build that focuses on flooding the board with tokens and using them to take out his opponents. Haste, lords, sacrifice outlets, and a few combos round out this very aggressive deck.

Current version: 1.0

Ryan's Yidris deck is wheels galore. Look out for the secret secondary commander, Nekusar , as Ryan cascades through his library. Good luck getting the timing right and catching all the cast triggers, though.
